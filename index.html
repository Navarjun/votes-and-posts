Votes & Posts — US Presidential Election Candidates

The United States' Presidential Election 2016, scheduled for Tuesday, November 8, 2016, will be the 58th quadrennial U.S. election. Voters will select presidential electors who in turn will elect a new president and vice president through the Electoral College. The series of presidential primary elections and caucuses is taking place between February 1 and June 14,2016 among the 50 states, the District of Columbia and U.S. territories.
